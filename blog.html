Dutchess

10 Pack


Genetics: Dutch treat X Alien Kush F2

Flowering Time: 10-11 weeks

I was gifted a very special DutchTreat No.5 a few years back... She has a rich limonene terp content and just wreaks. A stupefying Sativa that yields well but takes 11-12 weeks to finish and must be trellised. While it seemed an odd match the coupling has become a favorite among growers in our community. The resulting hybrid yields well, smells amazing and has better structure than the mon as well as shortened flower times.
